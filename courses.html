Courses We Offer

At our L.D. Patil Academy English Medium School Penguin Kindergarten, we offer a wide range of courses designed to provide students with a comprehensive education that focuses on developing their academic, social, and emotional skills. Our courses are designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a love for learning in a safe and nurturing environment.

We offer courses in English language, mathematics, science, social studies, physical education, music, art, and more. Our curriculum is designed to meet the highest educational standards and to prepare students for success in their academic and personal lives.

Our experienced and dedicated faculty members use a variety of teaching methods to cater to the individual needs of each student, ensuring that they receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for their future endeavors. At our L.D. Patil Academy English Medium School Penguin Kindergarten, we strive to create a positive and engaging learning environment that inspires students to become lifelong learners and responsible citizens.

RTE

LDP abids by the laws of Government and supports the Right to Education Act (RTE), 2009. We welcome the students allotted by the Government.

LEARNING PROGRAM

Maharashtra State Board learning program has been laid out for student from Class 1 to class 10. Exploring the world practicality and creativity. Teaching new concepts using practical models.

ACTIVITY

We at LPD are very passionate about enhancing the learning process through various platforms, where students can showcase their creative ideas. Activities provides an opportunity for all children to further develop their interactive skills, peers to accomplish a task.

SOCIAL ACTIVITY

We encourage students to not only understand empathy but also to give back to the society.

SPORTS PROGRAM

Sports is introduced with a vision to integrate a strong emphasis on sports and physical development. Students are encouraged to experience the challenge and reward the sport. It is designed to develop and enhance skills like strength, speed, flexibility, control, balance etc.
