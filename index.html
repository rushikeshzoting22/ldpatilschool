About

L.D Patil Academy English Medium School situated at Shivaji Nagar, Nashik was initiated in 2015. It is recognised by the Education Department of Maharashtra. The school campus is nestled in the nature, sprawling across 3 acres of land. This provides a eco-friendly and nature driven environment for students. It has spacious, airy classroom, playground, auditorium and knowledgable contents painted on the walls of the corridor.We aim to provide a safe learning environment with a welcoming atmosphere which creates a sense of belonging amongst the families. We maintain an inclusive environment which acknowledges and respects children from diverse family and cultural backgrounds..

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide exceptional care to children while fostering each child's intellectual,social,physicaland moral development in an academic-rich environment. Our mission is to work together to build a safe, respectful and nurturing environment focused on maximizing each child's sense of wellbeing and acquisition of skills for life learning

OUR VISION

LDP is committed to nuture each child to develop a curiosity of learning, discover their interests and grow in their love of learning We intend that all children should enjoy their learning,achieve their potential and become independent life-long learners. Our statement "DREAM, BELIEVE, ACHIEVE" reflects our understanding and beliefs.

Curriculum & Strategy

  • Integrated learning through LEAD School

  • Enable child to achieve national grade level proficiency

  • Build conceptual clarity in Math & Science through our concrete pictorial abstract approach
