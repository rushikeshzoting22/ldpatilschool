About Us

We are awesome TEAM

Building a great team is not easy! It takes time, patience and hard work. Leaders are frequently thinking about the health and performance of their teams by trying to understand how strong are the relationships within it. Many studies and research have shown that successful teams will nearly always outperform individuals working independently, especially in high-pressure scenarios. This should not come as much of a surprise, because most businesses are recognizing the value of team building in fostering winning cultures plus helping them to recruit talent which is becoming increasingly more competitive.


At LDP we Believe in a joyful experiential learning system wherein, each child is encouraged to participate wholeheartedly! While you enter a new academic year,besides learning and being good at subjects; learning the precious value of life is also equally important. At LDP, not only do we expect our children to grow academically but more importantly, we also work towards making them truly responsible Indian citizens. We believe that powerful learning and teaching occurs under a shared spirit of respect which creates a passionate schoolingexperience recognized for its warmth, energy and excellence. We foster a positive spirit and believe in partnership between students,parents,teachers and support staff striving to create a milieu that sustains excellence.
